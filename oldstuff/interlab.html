IrrE experiments

Our engineered bacteria will need to perform their tasks (e.g. produce light) in extreme conditions. For our gold medal, we improved the BBB biobrick by showing its function in simulated extreme conditions: high salinity. The IrrE biobrick, which gives bacteria the IrrE genes from Deinococcus Radiodurans was found to help our cells survive more than normal cells after 30 hours. This biobrick will help our cells survive when, for example, our light bulb would be cleaned or when in extreme Martian conditions.
