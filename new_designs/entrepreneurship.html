Light Induced TechnologiesLTD

Bringing urban design and synthetic biology together

At LIT, we develop applications for biological light switches – molecular devices that activate and suppress cellular processes. The LITbulb uses the daily cycles of day and night to regulate its bioluminescence via a genetically engineered circuit. Making use of bacterial co-culturing, the LIT bulb provides an eco-friendly, cost-effective and aesthetically pleasing solution that is sustainable for the long term to light up the streets of London.

About us

LIT, Light Induced Technologies, is a London based company that combines the concepts of Synthetic Biology with Urban Design to create innovative technologies that target current demand.

Currently, the world is in the process of rethinking 2 essential aspects of what our metropoles mean: energy consumption and the integration of nature into urban design.

We see the implementation of new and better technology to satisfy the needs for private and public illumination as the only solution to the issue. Not only will our products help with the current worldwide endeavour of reducing energy consumption, they will also make citizens more aware of what their surroundings are made of and how one must take care of that. The concept of the citizen can again be relieved.

First Stop: London

“Europe Strategy 2020” aims to cut CO2 emissions by 20% before 2020. In the UK, city councils are requested to implement measures that will reduce their carbon emissions. At the moment, 85% of the city councils dim or turn off the streetlights to save electricity costs and reduce their carbon foot print.

We envision a future where LIT bulbs will replace all streetlamps throughout the municipalities of the U.K. This would, among other things, mean there would be no need for cities to have to dim or turn off their street lights in order to comply with ultimatums to become greener and reduce their carbon emissions. Similarly, our LIT bulbs offer an innovative, low-cost and low-emission solution for combatting the high energy costs governments and municipalities incur annually to light up their streets. Our long-term future thinking includes making LIT bulbs that can be used for lighting billboards, gardens and outdoor venues.

Compartments and Leadership

Departmental Responsibilities

The department of finance and accounting will go through all operation process and manage the budget and spendings. We will collaborte with Intellecutal Property Rights Law Firms to file a patent for our product design. This way we will provide our LIT bulb with the maximal protection and ensure advantage in the market of streetlights. Most departments will either be involved in all production lines or 2 production lines. Except for Public Relations and Marketing which only require information about the final product. These departments will then be in charge of advertising the LIT bulb to the government/ local authorities or any other private construction companies. Human resources will be looking at the whole process and recruit employees whose profile and experience corresponds to the skills LIT requires. Safety and Quality control will overlook the production of bacterial content to ensure it meets government’s regulations of disposal and to ensure the streetlight design and manufacture comply with cGMP (current Good Manufacture Practices.) The R&D department will focus on biologics production and imporvement of the engineered E.coli strains, co-culture of the 2 micro-organisms. We will strive to incorporate customers' feedback and constantly improve the LIT bulb. Last but not least, logistics and assembly sub-division will be greatly involved in installing the streetlight at local community. They need to focus on the procedure of assembling each component.
